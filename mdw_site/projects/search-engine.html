Search Engine

A custom search engine that connects users with over 7 million results.

launch Launch the Prototype

Challenge

  • Update an existing interface without changing basic functionality
  • Use UI to help users better understand the existing hierarchy of results
  • Develop a card interface to allow for faster content browsing
  • Redesign advanced filter menus to allow for more intuitive narrowing of results
  • Highlight relevant content and make connections between related results, such as sites and site owners

Research

  • Search tool personae were identified and referenced throughout the duration of the project
  • A beta version of the new search interface was used to collect user feedback about the design
  • Collaborated closely with the global technology team to research new component functionality for incorporation in existing material design kit
  • Interviewed users to understand their experience using the new seach and identify areas for improvement before launch

My Role

  • Took the lead on all UI deliverable (mockups, prototypes, ecosystem, brand adherence)
  • Worked closely with global technology teams to prototype, design, and test new components for the company material design kit
  • Worked with a taxonomist to ensure menu accuracy
  • Audited all UX deliverables to optimize usability
  • Presented work to stakeholder groups
  • User testing via mockup walkthroughs, surveys, interviews, and a beta launch

Final Designs

The final result was clean, contemporary search interface that put a spotlight on content.

Cards nested within results pages surface relevant content. Cards help users distinguish between content types, such as social mentions and site results in the exampe above.

An alternative card interface for documents and people content makes it easier to browse results accurately.

News, cotacts, and sales content related to searches now appear on results pages. This helps users make connections between results and consider new avenues for sales.
