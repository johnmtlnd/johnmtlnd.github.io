UI that makes a complex tool simple to used on both mobile and desktop.

Overview

This expansive analytics platform included a custom survery design tool, a user portal, admin-only portals, and different data visualization tools. Final Designs

The final result was clean, contemporary search interface that put a spotlight on content.

The analytics platform allows users to build custom surveys using dozens of modular parts, track targets and responses, and filter massive sets of data. An easy-to-use backend was implemented to help users sort through all available options.

Multiple progress bars, steppers, and other visual signifiers guide users through the survey experience. Arranging these elements carefully ensures that they remain useful, not distracting.

Dozens of question types allow users to collect data about their audience. Each component was custom-designed with care to match company brand standards and work seamlessly across desktop and mobile. Component use and details were documented for immediate for inclusion in the company digital product design parts kit.

A user-friendly backend incorporates features such as drag-and-drop functionality to produce the best user experience. Extensive menus are thoughtfully separated based on function to help the user find the tools they need.
