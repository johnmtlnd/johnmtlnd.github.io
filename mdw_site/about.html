About Michael

Michael has a background in digital product design and offers professional UI/UX design services.

Experience

I’m Michael, a passionate and award winning UI/UX designer living in New York with 6+ years of design experience. I’ve studied design in 4 countries with a B.F.A in graphic design from Virginia Commonwealth University and have studied User Experience with the Nielsen Norman Group while in New York.

Interests

Throughout my design journey I have picked up on reading basic Spanish and French, visited 12 countries (studying design in 4 of them), serve as the co-chair of the Mercer Pride LGBTQIA+ group and a member of the Mercer Cares committee.

Outside of design, I have spent time volunteering in places like the lab for the world’s first underground park! I have a shameless obsession with French culture and Paris is the city of my dreams. I’m a patron of the ballet, theatre, and bad pop concerts.



Services Offered

Digital Product Design—Design Project Management—Website UI—Mobile App UI—UX Elevation—UI Re-Designs—UI/UX Design Training—Visual Design & Illustration
